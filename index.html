Focus Areas:

  • Don’t do repeat plays on CT side as it gives away your “positional advantage” and make it obvious for the enemies to counter you.
  • Don’t walk into fights whenever possible don’t walk in “danger” angles, these are angles where in your mind there is more than a 20% chance of an enemy being there.
  • Reloading in high conflict areas isn’t a good idea as it has a high chance of you getting killed while reloading, only try to reload when you are 100% safe from the enemy and ideally out of the distance where the enemy can hear you reload.
  • Whenever changing positions always keep your crosshair towards the position that poses the most threat to you even if this makes movement slightly more difficult as you could rather be looking at the danger than have to do a 180 turn to try get the kill.
  • For eco buys, don’t buy armour and default pistol (usp/glock) always upgrade your pistol which is your firepower and don’t buy head armour so the normal eco buy is: Deagle(or Tec9) + body armour.

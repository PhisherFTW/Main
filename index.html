Lesson 1 (Introduction Lesson/Live Analysis) 25/06/2023

Lesson video

Video

Focus Areas:

  • Don’t do repeat plays on CT side as it gives away your “positional advantage” and make it obvious for the enemies to counter you.
  • Don’t walk into fights whenever possible don’t walk in “danger” angles, these are angles where in your mind there is more than a 20% chance of an enemy being there.
  • Reloading in high conflict areas isn’t a good idea as it has a high chance of you getting killed while reloading, only try to reload when you are 100% safe from the enemy and ideally out of the distance where the enemy can hear you reload.
  • Whenever changing positions always keep your crosshair towards the position that poses the most threat to you even if this makes movement slightly more difficult as you could rather be looking at the danger than have to do a 180 turn to try get the kill.
  • For eco buys, don’t buy armour and default pistol (usp/glock) always upgrade your pistol which is your firepower and don’t buy head armour so the normal eco buy is: Deagle(or Tec9) + body armour.

FFA DM

  • Pistol FFA, solid crosshair placement but sometimes shooting slightly too quickly causing your deagle/usp to be inaccurate.
  • Rifle FFA, quick switching after every kill got you punished a few times, bursting is okay but sometimes you burst before your crosshair is on the enemy's head.
  • A lot of fights are taken when moving forward this causes you to be a little slow as you have to counter-strafe using the s key and I don’t think you are doing that as it looks like you are sliding to a stop, ideally 95% of your fights
  • Slight movement issues when moving around the map.

Focus Areas:

  • Buy body armour by default on both sides on Pistol round.
  • Only ever expose yourself to 1 angle at a time.
  • Whenever running long on Dust2 show your back towards long as you are running up to dodge any enemy flashes and throw a molly into the door-way to stop them from coming out.
  • When shooting at people boosting always shoot the one on the bottom at the one on top will drop causing them to be inaccurate longer allowing you to secure the kill on them.
  • Try to throw at little LOS (Line Of Sight) utility as possible as it only exposes you to danger, while you throw utility you could have probably thrown from a safer position.
  • Always try to position yourself close to cover so if you are caught slightly off-guard or flashed you have somewhere to fall back to I noticed a couple times on B you were exposed while throwing utility without any easily accessible cover.

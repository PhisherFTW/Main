Lesson 1 (Introduction Lesson/Live Analysis) 22/02/2024

Focus Areas

  • If using utility on Pistol rounds, use reactive utility instead of proactive utility, this will increase the chances of the utility having a meaningful impact on the round.
  • Make sure your utility lands where you expect it before relying on it to keep you safe for example a Molly/Smoke.
  • Always try to play within the optimal range of the weapon you are currently using although you might get kills in other situations by strictly playing within the optimal range of your weapon you increase you chances of winning every fight you take.
  • In a few rounds you threw LOS (Line of sight) utility which nearly and did get you punished a few times, whenever possible don't throw utility while being exposed to a potentially dangerous angle.
  • Try not to tunnel vision on players in certain positions as you play against better players they will use this tactic the bait and switch constantly, learning to manage your aggression when there is seemingly a free kill can help you win rounds.

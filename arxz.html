Lesson 1 (Introduction Lesson/Live Analysis) 08/07/2023

Focus areas

  • Buy body armour on pistol rounds, as these are very important rounds to try to win, and body armour is probably the best thing you can buy for 3 main reasons: 1) Having body armour reduces your aim punch—this is your character flinching when he gets shot, causing your crosshair to jump, which makes shooting very difficult. 2) Having body armour can significantly increase the amount of damage you can sustain to the stomach/chest; instead of being killed with 3 stomach shots, it can take between 6/7 shots to kill you like that. 3) Unlike utility, which is very easy to waste, you can’t really waste armour like you can with utility, and if you play well enough, you can carry it into the following round and make it cheaper for you.
  • Second round buys, if you win the first round, you put absolutely everything into the second round because you will always have an overall better buy than the enemy team, and this will give you a good advantage during this round. But if you don’t buy seriously, you can accidentally negate this advantage; obvious ways of doing this is just not using your money.
  • LOS utility, this was quite a common problem. This stands for Line Of Sight utility, and it's when you throw utility from out in the open and you can see where it lands, which isn’t usually good because that means an enemy could technically peek you while you have utility out.
  • During light buy/save rounds, your primary objective should be how can I do the most damage I can possibly do while spending as little as possible, and usually, this will be with an upgraded pistol like a p250/tec/fiveseven/cz. These weapons pack a huge punch for their price and at least give you a chance to secure a kill against a fully equipped opponent if you position yourself properly.
  • Optimal range, choose your position based on the weapon you can afford. If you can only afford an SMG/Shotgun, do not play them in the same position you would with a Rifle/Awp. This is the same for upgraded pistols; you want to play super close range in your first engagement for Upgraded pistols/SMGS and Shotguns due to accuracy fall off with range and damage fall off.

Lesson 2 (Live Analysis) 10/10/2023

Focus areas

  • Optimal range, this is the term I use to describe the range at which weapons are most deadly, for example AWP and Scout are best at long distance, Upgraded pistols/SMGS and Shotguns are best at super close range, I notice a few times during these matches you would often take fights outside of the optimal range when you were playing with Upgraded pistols and SMGS often making these fights a lot more difficult and in a lot of the cases being the main cause of your death.
  • Going for “unwinnable” retakes, I put that in quotes because although technically its winnable, its incredibly difficult situations like 2v4/2v5 situations when the enemies have the weaponry advantage / Positional advantage and the Time advantage, making the retake incredibly unlikely if they play at all decent.
  • Going for slightly obvious peeks, at times you were killed instantly because of the way you peeked an angle and this can be prevented by imagining what kind of peek the enemy is expecting you to do and then doing something different to that, which will give you a huge advantage in the fight and often throw off the enemies crosshair placement, if he is expecting a normal peek maybe do a jiggle and then a wide swing to draw in his crosshair.
  • Be wary of mollies at range, as enemies can often sit behind it making it very difficult for you to see them but easy for them to see you, this got you killed once but still something to take note of.
  • LOS utility, throwing potentially dangerous utility that leaves you exposed often with you seeing where it lands and thus allowing for an enemy to peek you on a timing while you have utility in your hand.
  • When on T side, be careful of taking unnecessary damage, because if your hp drops to 92, this allows M4’s to 1 hit headshot you, and this turns them into basically a better AK.
  • CT pistol round, I always recommend armour 99.5% of the time, this is for a number of reasons but the main ones are: 1) Having body armour reduces aim punch which in turn makes aiming easier and will probably result in you getting more kills 2) Body armour increases the number of body shots you can take significantly from 3 → 6/7 bodyshot which is a huge increase 3) You can’t “waste” body armour the same way you can miss a flash or a grenade and do nothing with it, if you play well enough it will even come with you into the following round making it cheaper!
  • Light buys, you want to do the most amount of damage for the least amount of money and you will not get that with utility the vast majority of the time unless you land grenades at the feet of multiple enemies it will almost always be better to choose a p250 or something similar as I always say bullets usually do more damage than utility.

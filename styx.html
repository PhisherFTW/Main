Lesson 1 (Introduction Lesson/Live Analysis) 14/10/2023

Focus Areas

  • Buy body armour on pistol rounds, as these are very important rounds to try win, and body armour is probably the best thing you can buy for 3 main reasons: 1) Having body armour reduces aim punch this is your character flinching when he gets shot this causes your crosshair to jump which makes shooting very difficult, 2) Having body armour can significantly increase the amount of damage you can sustain to the stomach/chest, instead of being kill with 3 stomach shots it can take between 6/7 shots to kill you like that. 3) Unlike utility which is very easy to waste, you can’t really waste armour like you can with utility and if you play well enough you can carry it into the following round and make it cheaper for you.
  • Using weapons at their optimal range, this is a term I use to describe the range at which weapons are most deadly like for example an AWP at long range and a shotgun at close range, now during this match I noticed when you played with the p250 you sometimes engaged with an enemy at a distance outside of the optimal range of pistol leading to incredibly difficult kills and often getting you in trouble.
  • Keep your position as “random” as possible to keep your positional advantage, whenever you force the enemy to look for you, you are giving yourself an advantage in that fight, but if the enemy already knows where you are from you throwing utility or making noise, this can be bad.
  • LOS utility, this got you in trouble a few times this game, this is the practise of throwing risky utility that could potentially lead to you dying to an enemy peeking on a good timing a good way of avoiding this is by focusing on always throwing utility from behind some sort of good cover (cover that can’t be spammed through).

Map: Mirage

Starting Side: CT

  1. Grenade, a weird choice of buy; usually, I recommend going with body armor as there are numerous advantages and it's very difficult to “waste” body armor. Solid grenade into site doing 44 damage to one enemy, unfortunately immediately after you threw it there was an engagement you were not really ready to take. L
  2. P250, now with upgraded pistol buys the most important thing is going to be your positioning, and unfortunately, your positioning this round wasn’t good. This led to the enemies immediately spotting you and removing your cover. When you are on a low buy, your first engagement must be super close range; this drastically improves your chances of landing a headshot and makes sure your weapon is doing its max damage. Also, during these rounds, you don’t want to play “standard” positions like headshot pillar; you want to be at an off-angle to try to catch the opponent off-guard. L

Bad timing on the decision to leave B site as they were coming out apps led to you being caught off-guard twice in a row, resulting in your death. L
  5. Five-seven buy, nice molly apps, and I like your thought processing with the smoke catwalk. Good movement around site after hearing an enemy drop apps in order to completely catch him off-guard. Really, really good patience on display here to wait for your teammates to peek from cat and take on the last enemy with your team. W

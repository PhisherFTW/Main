Focus Areas:

  • Don’t rely on crouching when engaging an enemy as it makes you a slow more obvious target, while not improving your accuracy unless you are tapping.
  • Try to focus on learning contact timings and the fastest possible position an enemy could be in this can reduce wasted utility and unnecessary angle clearing as well as allowing you to pull of slightly more risky plays.

  • Trigger discipline is a fantastic skills but its incredibly hard to hone as it comes to us so rarely you get very few chances to practise this, patience is key although always try to make the most of every situation as the difference between getting a single/double/triple kill when it comes to trigger discipline is often less than 4 seconds.
  • When choosing after-plant positions on T side there are a few factors I want you to think about, when playing to win a round we can do it in 2 ways, either the bomb explodes or you kill the entire enemy team, choose a position that will allow you to win in both ways, you can get some good peeks and you are not just stuck in an obvious corner, as well as playing time (Even better if you can drop utility on the bomb) some obvious examples for this are short on B OR Bathrooms on A in Overpass, these are great because it allows you to defend the bomb while still wasting time.

  • Learn new sprays for M4 and AK; you still look quite uncomfortable in medium-range engagements, often opting for bursting over spraying, which allows the enemy more time to react and slows down your time to kill.

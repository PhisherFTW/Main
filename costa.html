Lesson 1 (Demo Review) 18/01/2024

Focus Areas

  • Thow less LOS utility.
  • Take more time lining up smaller utility during this game I noticed 6 times that utility didn't land where you wanted and although that is not alot if this isn't addressed this will become a habit.
  • Don't peek an unknown angle with utility in your hand, and take every peek as if an enemy will be there.
  • When playing with a SMG play within its optimal range, numerous times during that match you took gun fights with MP9s and mac-10s with enemies very far away which caused these fights to be very inefficient.
  • I would recommend to try to identify key features of the rounds you win vs the rounds you lose, this will help you break a losing streak of rounds and help you adapt your style if you are having a winning streak.

Lesson 2 (Demo Review) 28/01/2024

Focus Areas

  • LOS utility again(Having utility in your hand when an enemy is peeking you)
  • Being exposed to multiple dangerous angles at once, try only to be exposed to one dangerous angle at a time.
  • Try to only take engagements with weapons at their “optimal” range for shotguns close range and awp/scout long.
  • Try using movement more to your advantage with jiggling and circle strafing to bait out shots and information.
  • When you think you are about to get peeked but you are forced into a corner play slightly more aggressive, this will give you the opportunity to use the angle more if there are multiple opponents as opposed to sitting completely in the corner and getting double peeked.

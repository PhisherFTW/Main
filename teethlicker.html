  • Always try to only engage one enemy at a time and take fights on as tight an angle as you can, there were a few situations that game where you overexposed yourself to a few enemies at once.
  • Try to use your CT side advantages whenever possible (Positional advantage / Advantage of holding) With Positional advantage, you want to keep your position hidden as long as possible in order to try to catch the enemy off-guard also changing your position basically every round ensures that you are not caught off-guard often, and Advantage of holding is mainly beneficial when you are holding an angle in which you can react to secure the kill, this is most effective when enemies are coming out of a tight chokepoint and to properly take advantage of this, make sure you don’t give them the site for free.

Lesson 3 (Live Analysis) 09/09/2024

Focus Areas

  • Spray control, I believe spraying will always be the superior shooting method in the vast majority of situations, as more of the common engagements you will take fall in medium - close range, which spraying excels, I have linked the spray master map which you said you already have incase its a different version, and you mentioned how you used to get high scores and how they have decreased, I want to include this into you training routine along with the Run/Stop/Flick/Spray FFA Deathmatch method.
  • When entrying onto site as a T, you want to spread out a little to make it as difficult as possible for the defending CT to get a multi kill, small changes in pathing can increase the likelihood of you being able to react to an enemy in an odd position greatly.
  • Whenever there is action before proceeding to a course of action make sure those actions will not negatively affect you, during this game there were a few times when you hyperfocused on an event and this led to you overlooking important angles or putting yourself at a disadvantage, try to evaluate your current situation before reacting to events.
  • Always try to force engagements within your weapons optimal range, there were a few times where you peeked with an SMG at a range outside of throwing distance and this is never advisable as it puts you in a huge disadvantage in this engagement, always position yourself in angles with suit you weapon.
  • Positioning is one of the most important things in this game, if you have bad positioning you will constantly be putting yourself in disadvantageous fights, if you have other methods of clearing angles like mollies I would highly recommend using it even if there is no-one there its not a waste, as the alternative is manually clearing the angle in which case you might lose the 1v1 in a 50:50 fight and if you do clear an angle that exposes you and there is no-one there, now you are exposed to other angles which is very dangerous.
